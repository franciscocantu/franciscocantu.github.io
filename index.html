Francisco Cantú


Political Science

University of Houston



Welcome! My research explores questions regarding political institutions and elections. I teach courses on comparative politics, political economy, and data science. My work has been published in the American Journal of Political Science, American Political Science Review, Journal of Politics, and Political Analysis, among others. I work as an Associate Professor of Political Science at the University of Houston and hold a Ph.D. from the University of California, San Diego.
