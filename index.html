Francisco Cantú


Political Science

University of Houston



I work as an Associate Professor of Political Science at the University of Houston. My interests lie in broad areas of comparative politics, political economy, and quantitative methods. My work explores questions regarding political institutions, elections, and corruption. My research has been published in the American Journal of Political Science, American Political Science Review, Journal of Politics, British Journal of Political Science, and Political Analysis, among others. I received a Ph.D. from the University of California, San Diego.
